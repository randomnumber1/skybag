 IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
 PROGRAM-ID. PROG01.
 DATA DIVISION.
 WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
 01 GENDER          PIC X(1).
     88 BOY         VALUE 'B'.
     88 GIRL        VALUE 'G'.
 PROCEDURE DIVISION.
     DISPLAY 'FIRST LETTER OF GENDER'
     ACCEPT GENDER
     EVALUATE TRUE
     WHEN BOY
             DISPLAY 'ITS A BOY'
     WHEN GIRL
             DISPLAY 'ITS A GIRL'
     WHEN OTHER
             DISPLAY 'ITS MANJEET'
     END-EVALUATE
     STOP RUN.
